When hovering over a country the color of that country changes and the population of that country is displayed. That is if there is any information to be displayed. The general color scheme already gives an idication of how many people live in that country and if you want the know the population exactly you can find out by hovering over it!

Click on a country to find out its population respectively to the rest of the world. Below you can choose if you prefer a barchart visualisation or a piechart visualisation which will appear under the right heading when a country is clicked.
